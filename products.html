Our Signature Pizzas

Classic Margherita – Fresh mozzarella, tomato sauce, and basil.

Pepperoni Overload – A cheesy delight topped with premium pepperoni.

BBQ Chicken – Grilled chicken, BBQ sauce, red onions, and cilantro.

Appetizers & Sides

Mozzarella Sticks – Golden-fried sticks of mozzarella cheese served with marinara sauce.

Buffalo Wings – Spicy, tangy chicken wings served with a side of ranch or blue cheese.

Pasta Dishes

Fettuccine Alfredo – Creamy Alfredo sauce served over fettuccine pasta with parmesan cheese.

Spaghetti & Meatballs – Classic tomato sauce with homemade meatballs over spaghetti.
