Paula and Paul's Pizza Palooza started in 2005 as a small family-owned restaurant with a passion for great pizza!

Our story began with a dream to bring authentic flavors with the freshest ingredients to the local community. Over the years, we have grown into a pizza favorite known not just for our amazing pizzas but also for our friendly atmosphere.

Our Journey

  1. 2005: Opened our first restaurant in New York.
  2. 2010: Expanded to five more locations.
  3. 2015: Launched our exclusive range of homemade sauces.
  4. 2020: Started nationwide delivery.
